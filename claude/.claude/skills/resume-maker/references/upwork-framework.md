# Upwork proposals

Upwork proposals are not cover letters.

The goal is to make the client think, this person has solved my problem before.

## five-part structure

1. hook
2. mirror their problem
3. proof
4. approach list
5. closing question

## hook

Open with a real moment, a specific problem, or a concrete discovery.

Do not open with generic enthusiasm.

## mirror the problem

Paraphrase the client's need accurately enough to prove you read the posting.

## proof

Use two or three strong evidence points with metrics, tools, and scale.

## approach list

Use a short numbered list.

Keep it outcome-focused, not process-heavy.

## close with a question

End with a real project question or next step.

## title formula

```text
[role] who [best metric] with [tool or method]
```

## handling skill gaps

1. acknowledge briefly
2. pivot to transferable proof
3. explain why the principle still carries over

## banned phrases

- I am excited to apply
- I am the perfect candidate
- results-driven professional
- passionate team player
- looking forward to hearing from you
